Process Outsource

Process Outsource, commonly referred to as Business Process Outsourcing (BPO), is the practice of delegating specific business processes or tasks to external service providers. These service providers are specialized in the respective processes and can handle them more efficiently, allowing companies to focus on their core competencies.

Process Outsource Include


CUSTOMER SUPPORT

Outsourcing customer support services involves handling customer inquiries, resolving issues, providing technical support, and managing customer interactions through various channels such as phone, email, live chat, or social media

HUMAN RESOURCES (HR) SERVICES

HR outsourcing can cover a range of functions including recruitment and staffing, employee onboarding, benefits administration, performance management, training and development, and HR compliance.

DATA ANALYTICS

Outsourcing data analytics services can involve tasks such as data collection, data cleansing, data mining, statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and generating actionable insights from large volumes of data.

DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT

Document management services involve organizing, digitizing, indexing, storing, and retrieving documents and records, both in physical and digital formats. This can include document scanning, data capture, document storage, and document retrieval.

Other Outsource services

Data Entry and Data Processing: Data entry services involve accurately inputting and processing data into digital systems, databases, or spreadsheets. This includes tasks like data transcription, data cleansing, data validation, and data formatting.

Accounting and Bookkeeping: Outsourcing accounting and bookkeeping processes can involve tasks such as accounts payable and receivable management, financial statement preparation, payroll processing, tax preparation, and general ledger maintenance.

IT Services: IaC is an approach that treats infrastructure configuration, provisioning, and management as code. It involves using declarative or imperative code to define and automate the provisioning and configuration of infrastructure resources, such as servers, networks, and storage. IaC enables consistent, reproducible, and scalable infrastructure deployments, reducing manual errors and facilitating collaboration

Content Moderation: Content moderation services involve reviewing and moderating user-generated content across various platforms to ensure compliance with guidelines, community standards, and regulatory requirements. This includes moderating social media content, online forums, comments sections, and user reviews.

Procurement and Supply Chain Management: Outsourcing procurement and supply chain processes can include supplier management, vendor selection, purchase order management, inventory management, logistics coordination, and demand forecasting.
